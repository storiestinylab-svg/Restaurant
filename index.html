Our Story

A Kitchen Built on Love & Tradition

Founded in 2008 by Chef Kabir Rahman, Royal Taste began as a small family kitchen with a single vision: serve food so good that guests feel like they're dining at home. Today, we serve over 500 guests daily while keeping that same soul.

100% Fresh Ingredients

Sourced daily from local farms. No frozen, no artificial flavoring — ever.

Hygienic Kitchen Standards

Our kitchen meets Grade A hygiene certification. Open kitchen policy — see your food being made.

Fast & Warm Service

Every order served within 15 minutes. Because your time matters as much as your taste.
